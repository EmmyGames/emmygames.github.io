Sheep Herding

Play as a sheepdog herding sheep into a pen in order to score points and regain time. Get as many points as possible before time runs out. There are also special sheep (black and gold) that reward more points or more time!

Sheep Herding Game was a solo project I worked on when I had just started learning Unity. I am proud of the gameloop it has and the overall flow of the game. I am not very good at aesthetic related things so all the art assets were sourced from the Unity asset store. It was an early project that displays animations, AI, and a full game loop with a main menu and loss screen that lets you play again.
