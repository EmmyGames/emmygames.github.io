BUG OFF

Squash the massive alien bug invasion by responding to cities in danger in this 3D tower defense game. Strategically choose, place, and upgrade your towers to stop the bugs from winning as they aim to destroy your defenses and base of operations!

BUG OFF started as a prototype. I taught a Game Design & Programming 1 Summber class as a TA under Matthew Johnson at Marist College. The class was taught in Godot and was the catalyst for teaching myself Godot. At the end of the class, I decided to make a simple tower defense game to combine my recent knowledge. After making the prototype, I thought it was actually fun and brainstormed the rest of the design before making a steam page for the game.
